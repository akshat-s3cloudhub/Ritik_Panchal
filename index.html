About DG Panchal

Four decades of precision engineering excellence

Legacy of Excellence Since 1983

DG Panchal - DG Group has been at the forefront of precision engineering and gear manufacturing for over four decades. Established in 1983, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality industrial components and machinery that meet the most demanding specifications.

Our commitment to innovation, accuracy, and durability has made us a trusted partner for industries across India and beyond. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver solutions that exceed expectations.
