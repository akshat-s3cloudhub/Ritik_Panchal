Product Description

Our High-Precision Worm Gears are designed for applications requiring high reduction ratios and self-locking capabilities. The unique screw-like worm design meshes with a worm wheel to provide significant speed reduction in a compact package, while the inherent self-locking feature prevents back-driving.

These specialized gears are ideal for lifting equipment, positioning systems, and applications where holding position under load is crucial. Our worm gears are manufactured to precise tolerances using premium materials for long-lasting performance.

Key Features

  • Self-locking capability prevents back-driving
  • High reduction ratios in compact design
  • Smooth and quiet operation
  • Excellent load-holding capabilities
  • Single or multi-start worm configurations
  • Bronze or steel worm wheel options
