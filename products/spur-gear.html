Product Description

Our High Precision Spur Gears represent the cornerstone of mechanical power transmission systems. Manufactured with over four decades of expertise, these straight-tooth gears are designed for parallel shaft applications where reliability and precision are paramount.

Each spur gear is precision-machined using advanced CNC technology and quality-tested to ensure optimal performance. The straight-cut teeth provide efficient power transmission with minimal noise and vibration, making them ideal for a wide range of industrial applications.

Key Features

  • Precision-cut straight teeth for optimal mesh
  • Superior material quality and heat treatment
  • Consistent tooth profile and spacing
  • Available in various modules and sizes
  • Custom specifications available
  • Suitable for high-speed applications
