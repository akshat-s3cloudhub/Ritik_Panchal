Product Description

Our High-Precision Bevel Gears are expertly engineered for intersecting shaft applications where power transmission must change direction. These conical-shaped gears feature precisely cut teeth that mesh at 90-degree angles, making them ideal for differential systems, steering mechanisms, and complex machinery applications.

Manufactured using advanced CNC machining technology and premium-grade materials, our bevel gears deliver exceptional performance under high-load conditions. The specialized tooth geometry ensures smooth power transmission while minimizing backlash and vibration.

Key Features

  • Conical design for intersecting shaft applications
  • Precision-machined tooth profiles for optimal meshing
  • High torque transmission capability
  • Minimal backlash and vibration
  • Available in straight and spiral tooth designs
  • Custom ratios and configurations available

Applications

Automotive Differentials

Essential component in vehicle differential systems for wheel speed control

Machine Tools

Direction change mechanisms in precision manufacturing equipment

Industrial Machinery

Power transmission in heavy-duty industrial applications

Marine Propulsion

Drive systems for marine vessels and propulsion equipment
