Product Description

Our high-precision helical gears are designed for applications requiring smooth operation and high load-carrying capacity. The angled teeth of helical gears provide gradual engagement, resulting in quieter operation and superior load distribution compared to spur gears.

Key Features

  • Angled tooth design for smooth engagement
  • Reduced noise and vibration
  • Superior load distribution
  • Higher load-carrying capacity
  • Available in left-hand and right-hand helix
  • Precision manufactured for optimal performance

Applications

Heavy Machinery

Industrial equipment requiring high torque transmission

Automotive Transmissions

Vehicle transmission systems for smooth gear changes

Gearbox Systems

High-performance gearboxes and reducers

Wind Turbines

Power transmission in renewable energy systems

Technical Specifications

Material EN8, EN24, C45
Module Range 1 to 12
Helix Angle 15° to 45°
Accuracy Grade DIN Class 6-8
