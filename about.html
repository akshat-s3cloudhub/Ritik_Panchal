About DG Panchal Four Decades of Engineering Excellence

Since 1983, we have been pioneers in precision engineering, delivering innovative solutions that drive industrial excellence across diverse sectors.

Our Legacy of Innovation

DG Panchal - DG Group has been at the forefront of precision engineering and gear manufacturing for over four decades. Founded in 1983, we have evolved from a small precision workshop to a comprehensive industrial engineering company.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide precision-engineered solutions that meet the highest quality standards. Today, we stand as a testament to innovation, reliability, and excellence in the manufacturing industry.

With state-of-the-art CNC and VMC machinery, skilled craftsmen, and an unwavering commitment to quality, we have successfully served industries ranging from textile and ginning to heavy machinery and automotive sectors.

Our Journey Through Time

Milestones that define our commitment to excellence

1983

Foundation

DG Panchal - DG Group was established with a vision to provide precision engineering solutions to the growing industrial sector in Gujarat.

1990

Gear Manufacturing

Expanded into comprehensive gear manufacturing, specializing in spur, helical, bevel, and worm gears for various industrial applications.

2000

CNC Technology

Invested in advanced CNC and VMC machinery to enhance precision and production capacity, setting new industry standards.

2010

Quality Certification

Achieved industry quality certifications and expanded into textile machinery components and ginning machine spare parts.

2020

Market Expansion

Extended our reach to serve clients across India and began exploring international markets with our precision engineering solutions.

2025

Future Vision

Continuing innovation with Industry 4.0 technologies and sustainable manufacturing practices for the next generation.
